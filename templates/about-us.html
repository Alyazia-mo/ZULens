About Us

Mission

ZULens objective is to empower students by providing a platform to share their academic experiences with AI-driven analytics. Our goal is to bridge the knowledge gap by enabling students to give transparent, structured, and unbiased reviews of their professors and courses. Through powerful NLP and Sentiment Analysis, we ensure that student feedback is organized and impactful, helping both students and instructors improve the learning experience.

Let's Help You Choose

Your choice of professor and course can shape your academic journey. At ZULens, we provide a space where students can openly share their insights, helping future students make informed decisions while giving professors valuable feedback to refine their teaching methods.

Why Choose ZULens?

Share your experience with a professor and their teaching style.
Help fellow students choose the right courses and instructors.
Ensure feedback is data-driven, transparent, and unbiased.
Support faculty in enhancing their teaching based on student input.
